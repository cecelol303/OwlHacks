Why I made this website?

As the daughter of a nurse, I was raised to take my health seriously and visit the doctor regularly. I believe everyone should have access to healthcare, regardless of insurance status. Sexual health, in particular, should be a key focus in health education for college students. There's no reason why students shouldn't be informed or unsure of where to seek help. That's why I created this site.
